The Problem

The internet made everything available, but not everything affordable.

E-commerce is a $5 trillion supermassive global market steeped in inefficiencies. Two identical sofas might sell for $3,200 at one retailer and $1,150 at another – same factory, same SKU, different logo. This is true in home goods, fashion, accessories, and beauty among other categories.

Most people don't realize they're overpaying for the same product listed under different brands or retailers. E-commerce is fragmented, prices are inconsistent, and shoppers waste hours hunting for deals.

Retail has become a confidence game, one where brand storytelling hides margin storytelling. Retail markups still dominate because there's no transparent price discovery layer for products, leading to increasing buyer regret post purchase. Consumers are now living in a constant state of FOMO before checkout and buyer's remorse after, and it's only getting worse.

The Solution: Dupe is to AI commerce what Stripe is to payments

Dupe solves the two most pernicious problems in ecommerce: consumer price discovery and minimizing buyer regret.

We find identical or near-identical products across the web at incredible deals, often 60-70-80% less than the product a shopper was just looking at.

The Market Opportunity

  • 57% of Gen Z already use AI to shop
  • 44% say it's their preferred way to shop
  • Millions are asking AI what to buy, every day

But there's a blind spot: LLMs don't understand commerce. They don't have product catalogs, behavioral data, or monetization rails. They can talk about shopping, not participate in it.

That's the white space Dupe is bridging over.

In addition to scaling our viral consumer platform at dupe.com, we've shipped and are actively piloting an MCP (Mode Context Protocol) and API layer that lets any major LLM or e-commerce app plug directly into our infrastructure, enabling true agent-to-agent commerce.

For example, ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Claude could instantly pull live Dupe data to surface verified lookalikes and checkout links optimized for conversion – Dupe has behavioral and checkout data; they don't.

Think: instant access to live product data, price comparisons, and frictionless checkout links, all wrapped in a 95% gross-margin monetization rail.

But information alone isn't enough to fix incentives. To realign incentives between shoppers, partners, and the platform, we need a shared economic layer.

That's where the $DUPE token comes in.

When fully integrated, this flywheel will be the first of its kind to ecommerce.

Key Mechanisms

  • Token Rewards: $DUPE is earned for taking deep actions with Dupe, ex: signing up, inviting friends, running deal searches, clicking partner links, installing our app, and having a daily streak on our platform
  • Token Burns: Every redeemed or boosted token gets burned from supply – making $DUPE scarcer. Token burns will be turned on at a future date (to be disclosed)
  • Token Buybacks: When users earn $DUPE through rewards, the platform buys those tokens back from the market into the $DUPE treasury. Dupe has already bought back 23% of supply to date
