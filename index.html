Welcome to ICM Analytics

ICM Analytics is your source for data-driven insights for Internet Capital Markets (ICM). We empower YOU to uncover undervalued gems, start believing in projects, and enable fundamental investing in Crypto.

Our Mission & Goals

ICM Analytics has the following goals:

  • 1. Make people believe: with a comprehensive deep-dive for selected projects with fundamental metrics
  • 2. Data-Driven approach: educate people on the metrics relevant to the project (this could be P/E, GMV, et cetera)
  • 3. Integrate those metrics into a dashboard: so investors can easily manage their investment by watching growth metrics
